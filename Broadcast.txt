At 9:52 p.m. Pacific Daylight Time Tuesday, Environment Canada has issued a severe thunderstorm warning for Southwest Interior British Columbia. Extra care and attention should be exercised in and around the following localities: Shuswap. Environment Canada meteorologists are tracking a line of severe thunderstorms capable of producing heavy rain. Take cover immediately if threatening weather approaches. Intense lightning is likely and tornados are possible with any thunderstorm that develops.
